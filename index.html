Sabrina Carpenter is a musician who started her career on Disney Channel. Her first album released was called Eyes Wide Open in 2015 and featured songs like Can't Blame a Girl for Trying, which you can listen to a snippet to by clicking the Eyes Wide Open album cover below!

Sabrina Carpenter's music has gained popularity over the past few years as she had released multiple songs that went viral on social media platform TikTok. Earlier in 2023, she even joined Taylor Swift as an opener for her Eras Tour. Her album Emails I Can't Send was her most popular release, with songs like Nonsense and Feather that brough upbeat tempos.

Sabrina Carpenter's music is catchy and fun to listen to.
